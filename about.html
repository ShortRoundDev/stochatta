What does "stochatta" mean?

As far as I can tell, nothing. It was the name of a composition in the 1963 album Music From Mathematics, one of the first digital music albums ever produced. Though the composition was named Stochatta, the name would have been better reserved for a later track on the album, Improvisations On A Random Piano, in which composers were given a half measure of piano music and asked to compose another half measure in response. That composer's half measure was then given to another composer, and so on. The number of half measures available to a composer increased with each round, until 3 half measures were available. This is, of course, a Markov chain in action, and would have more appropriately been named Stochatta. The actual Stochatta is here
